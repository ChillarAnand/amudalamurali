Amudala Murali

Amudala Murali is an avadhani, poet, writer and lecturer. He performed avadhanam at serveral places in India. He wrote 6 books. His poems and articles were published in several magazines. He works as a lecturer at Vijaya Krishna Jr. Collge, Tirupati.
